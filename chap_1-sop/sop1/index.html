The management of the college provides a good quality teaching and learning environment to strengthen the character and personality of the students through –
1)Equipping the student with relevant knowledge with ICT and holistic development methods in order to face global challenges.
2)Provision of modern educational facilities to the students.
3)Making students aware of human rights, value system, cultural, scientific temperament and environmental issues.
* The college runs under the aegis of Arihant Education foundation, Pune was established in the year 2012-13 and is located at Bavdhan, suburban area of Pune Municipal Corporation, Pune. The College has state of arts infrastructure with ICT enabled laboratories and classroom. The college runs senior college programs in all the streams viz. Arts, Commerce and Science
